## Overview

FindHelp is a side project I built to help people in Taiwan discover welfare and subsidy programs offered by different cities and counties.

## Challenges

- **Scattered data sources**: Government websites use different formats, which makes collection and maintenance harder.
- **Search experience**: Finding the right policy with intuitive keywords took several iterations.
- **Keeping content fresh**: Welfare policies change over time, so the content needs regular review.

## Tech Stack

Built with Next.js and Tailwind CSS, deployed on Vercel for fast iteration and sharing.
